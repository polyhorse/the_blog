polyhorse
Making games should be easy

We're a team of loose cannons making AAA tools for commercial game engines.

Our captain may have made us turn in our badges, but we're determined to clean up game-dev city. If you're game creator, we will hunt you, we will find you, and then we'll politely offer you a discount code to one of our plugins.

Made for Unity

We enjoy digging deep into the gritty innards of Unity. We've read all the docs, and we've mastered the workflow.

Premium UX

We make our UI sexy like a flutist. We combine years of experience with constant user testing, making sure our tools will save time, and bring a smile to your face.

Responsive

We're dedicated to the proposition that relationships are as important as the product, so we're on call to help you every waking minute.

Built with Love

No shit. We love what we do. To us, making tools for game-making is more fun than making games.

The best, time-saving workflows have a balance of good features,
performant code, and are a joy to use.
